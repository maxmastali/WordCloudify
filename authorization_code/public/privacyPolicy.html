WordCloudify

Welcome to WordCloudify

WordCloudify allows Spotify users to display their top artists in the form of a word cloud. You can customize your word cloud by choosing your word cloud's color theme as well as time range of your top artist data.

For more detail on how WordCloudify uses your data, please check out our privacy policy.

Privacy Policy

WordCloudify uses Spotify's API in order to retrieve your top artists. By choosing to use WordCloudify, you agree to the use of the data for your top artists.

None of the data gathered by WordCloudify is stored or shared to any third parties. All information collected is used solely to present your word cloud.

If at any point you would like to revoke WordCloudify's permissions with your account, you can do so on your apps page. This article provides further instructions on how to do so.
